If $S$ is a compact, orientable surface without boundary, then
$$
\int_S K=2\pi\,\chi(S),
$$
where $K$ is the Gaussian curvature of $S$ and $\chi(S)$ its 
\PMlinkname{Euler-Poincar\'e characteristic.}{EulerrCharacteristic}
